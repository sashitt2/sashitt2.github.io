About me

I am a graduate student in the Aerospace Engineering Department at UIUC, pursuing a PhD under the guidance of Prof. Daniel Bodony. I am also completing a Masters in Compuer Science at UIUC under the guidance of Prof. Mohammed El-Kebir. Prior to this, I graduated with a B.Tech in Aerospace Engineering from Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Bombay, and was fortunate to have worked with Prof. Krishnendu Sinha and Prof. Johan Larsson during my time there.

Research

Broadly speaking, my current research interests lie in formulating and solving optimization and inference problems with biomedical/healthcare applications. I especially prefer to work on problems that require a combination of ideas from mathematics and computer science.
